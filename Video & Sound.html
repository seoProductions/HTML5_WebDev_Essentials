HTML5 Video

Prior to HTML5, developers would rely on external tools to display video on the interwebs. Luckily, HTML5 has brought the functionality needed to support playing Video

Here is an early experiment I made a while back. I was simply playing around with Sand Kinematics and C++

NOTE: if the video fails to load. The browser will show the text inbetween the video tags

NOTE: most HTML5 video attributes do not need values. They are more like flags

Let's look at some possible video flags

  • src - source of the video
  • width, height - helps page load faster
  • poster - (FLAG) - Display a picture as placeholder
  • controls - (FLAG) - tells browser to supply controlls for playback
  • autoplay - (FLAG)
  • loop - (FLAG) - loop the video
  • preload - What to do when the page loads
    • none - don't load video until user click on the video
    • auto - load video once the page loads
    • metadeta - browser can collect info.

HTML5 also provides the source tag - inorder to provide multiple video formats.
